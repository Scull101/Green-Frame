Our Story

Frames
That
Matter

Green Frame was founded by John Scully — an Irish photographer and filmmaker who brought his creative eye from Ireland to Abu Dhabi. The name combines green, a symbol of heritage and growth, with frame, representing the lens through which stories are told.

Today we're a collective of creatives, strategists, and collaborators who believe visuals can shift how people see the world. From corporate films to social content, we're united by one vision: to frame stories that make an impact.
